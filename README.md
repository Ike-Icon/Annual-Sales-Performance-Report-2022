# Zues Clothing Line: Annual Sales Performance Dashboard

An Excel dashboard analyzing 2022 sales performance for a retail clothing 
business, covering 31,237 orders across multiple channels, categories, 
and customer segments.

## Overview
Zues Clothing Line generated $21,176,377 in revenue from 31,237 orders 
in 2022. This project breaks down that performance by month, category, 
sales channel, and customer demographics to surface where revenue comes 
from and where the business is leaking money.

## Key Findings

- **Revenue**: $21.18M total, average order value $682.07
- **Peak season**: March ($1.93M / 2,819 orders), strong through May
- **Decline**: Steady drop from June through November
- **Customer base**: Women drive 64% of revenue, Adults are the top 
  age group across genders
- **Top channels**: Amazon and Myntra lead, Nalli and smaller channels 
  contribute minimally
- **Top category**: Sets generate over $10M, nearly double the next 
  category (Sarees)
- **Fulfillment**: Vast majority of orders delivered successfully, with 
  a small share cancelled, returned, or refunded

## Recommendations

1. Target adult women in marketing, they drive 64% of revenue
2. Prioritize spend on Amazon and Myntra, the top two channels
3. Promote Sets and Kurtas, the highest-selling categories
4. Run a mid-year promotion in Q3, sales drop off starting in June
5. Investigate cancellation and return rates to protect net revenue
